Mobile • Sports

Shove.bet

A live betting platform focused on clear and fast user interactions.

The Problem

Live betting platforms are often cluttered, confusing, and overwhelming for casual bettors. The sheer volume of data and complex navigation paths create friction, leading to missed betting opportunities and user frustration. Shove.bet needed a streamlined experience that prioritized speed and clarity.

My Role

Lead Product Designer UX Research & Strategy UI Design & Prototyping Interaction Design

The Process

01. Research

Analyzed competitor platforms and conducted user interviews to understand pain points.

02. Ideation

Sketched rapid wireframes focusing on the bet slip and live odds display.

03. Design

Developed a high-fidelity design system that ensures readability in high-stress moments.

04. Testing

Validated the simplified bet flow through usability testing, refining interactions.

Key Decisions

Simplified Bet Slip Interaction

Instead of a complex modal or a separate page, we introduced a bottom-sheet bet slip that overlays the live action. This allows users to place bets rapidly without losing sight of the game.

Dynamic Odds Highlighting

To reduce cognitive load, we implemented a subtle color-coding system (green for rising, red for falling odds) that helps users quickly parse changes without reading every number.

Focused Information Architecture

We stripped away secondary statistics from the main view, prioritizing current score, time remaining, and primary betting markets. Deeper stats are tucked behind a simple swipe gesture.

Final Design

The Impact

The redesign significantly improved the platform's core metrics. The streamlined interface allowed users to make decisions faster and with greater confidence.

40% Increase in bet placement speed
25% Reduction in abandoned bet slips
